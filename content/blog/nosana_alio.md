As agentic LLMs become more autonomous, securing them becomes critical.

Alio is an independent AI security lab building **Alio Firewall**, a system designed to detect, evaluate, and mitigate risks in agentic LLMs. Their work depends on running large-scale simulations, red-teaming exercises, and model validation pipelines, all of which require reliable, scalable GPU compute.

To support this workload, Alio partnered with **Nosana GPU Cloud**, gaining access to on-demand, cost-efficient GPUs without long-term infrastructure commitments.

### The Challenge: Scaling AI Security Workloads

Building AI security infrastructure is compute-intensive by nature.

Alio needed to:

* Train and validate LLM risk detection models  
* Run large-scale red-teaming simulations  
* Stress-test agentic behaviors across multiple scenarios  
* Scale workloads dynamically based on testing demand

Traditional cloud solutions made this difficult due to **high costs**, **rigid pricing**, and **limited flexibility** for burst workloads.
